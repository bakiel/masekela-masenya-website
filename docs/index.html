Who We Are

Your Legal Partner,
Since 2012

Masekela Masenya Inc is a premier full-service law firm in Pretoria, founded on the principle that every client deserves exceptional representation and results that matter.

Led by a Founding Director with an LLB from the University of Limpopo, and experience at Gildenhuys Lessing Malatji Attorneys and a former legal advisory role at Outsurance, we combine deep legal expertise with sound business acumen.

With 308 High Court appearances and an 82% success rate, our track record speaks for itself. We serve individuals, families and corporations from our Pretoria offices with equal dedication.

What We Do

Areas of Legal Practice

Nine specialist areas of law, handled by a team equipped to protect your rights and advance your interests at every stage.

01

Commercial Law

Contracts, company formation, shareholder disputes, mergers and acquisitions, and ongoing corporate governance for SMEs and large enterprises.

02

Family Law

Divorce proceedings, antenuptial contracts, maintenance, custody arrangements, and adoption. Sensitive counsel during life's most challenging moments.

03

Labour Law

CCMA representation, unfair dismissal, retrenchments, employment contracts and workplace dispute resolution for both employers and employees.

04

Medical Malpractice

Holding healthcare providers accountable for negligence. Expert claims handling for patients who have suffered harm due to medical error.

05

Estate Law

Will drafting, estate administration, deceased estate distribution, and trust formation. Protecting your legacy and your family's future.

06

Criminal Law

Bail applications, criminal trial representation, High Court appearances, plea negotiations and parole applications. Vigorous defence of your rights.

07

Third-Party Claims

Road accident fund claims, personal injury litigation, and third-party insurance claims. Getting you the compensation you deserve.

08

Consumer Protection

CPA compliance, consumer disputes, credit agreements, and debt review matters. Protecting your rights as a consumer under South African law.

09

Insurance Law

Policy interpretation, claim disputes, short-term and life insurance matters, and insurer litigation. We know insurance law and we know how to win.
