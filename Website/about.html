Our Journey

From Vision to
Verified Results

Masekela Masenya Inc was founded on the conviction that every South African deserves access to excellent legal representation - regardless of the complexity of their matter or the power of their opponent.

2012
The Foundation

Masekela Masenya established the firm after completing his LLB at the University of Limpopo and gaining invaluable experience at Gildenhuys Lessing Malatji Attorneys. The founding principle was simple: put the client first, always.

2014-2016
Building Expertise

The practice expanded its expertise across commercial, family, and labour law - gaining recognition for aggressive representation and nuanced understanding of South African legal frameworks. High Court appearances became routine.

2018-2020
Broadening the Practice

New specialisations in medical malpractice, third-party RAF claims, and insurance law expanded the firm's reach. The director's Certificate in Practice Management and PDBM brought sharper business systems to the legal practice.

2022-Present
MBA and Strategic Growth

The director's MBA reinforced a business-minded approach to legal problem solving. Today, Masekela Masenya Inc combines legal excellence with strategic business thinking - giving clients a decisive edge in every matter.
