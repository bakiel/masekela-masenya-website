Contact Us
How much does a consultation cost?

Initial consultation fees vary depending on the nature and complexity of your matter. Contact us to discuss your specific situation and we will provide a transparent fee structure upfront. No hidden costs.

How long does a legal matter typically take?

Timelines depend entirely on the type and complexity of the matter. Simple matters may resolve in weeks; complex litigation can take months to years. We provide realistic timeline estimates at your initial consultation.

Do you handle matters outside Pretoria?

Yes. While we are based in Pretoria, we handle matters across Gauteng and nationally. High Court appearances allow us to represent clients in cases throughout South Africa.

What documents should I bring to my first consultation?

Bring any documents relevant to your matter (contracts, correspondence, court papers, police reports, etc.), your ID document, and a brief written summary of your situation if possible. Do not worry if you don't have everything - we can guide you on what is needed.

Is my consultation confidential?

Absolutely. Attorney-client confidentiality is a fundamental legal obligation. Everything you discuss with us is completely confidential and protected by legal professional privilege.
