Every case is personally led. Every argument, personally crafted. Every victory, personally delivered. No junior attorneys. No hand-offs. Just direct, decisive legal representation from a Founding Director with over 308 High Court appearances behind him.

Masekela Masenya is the Founding Director of Masekela Masenya Inc, a premier Pretoria law firm that he established in 2012 after completing his LLB at the University of Limpopo.

Prior to founding the firm, he gained invaluable experience at Gildenhuys Lessing Malatji Attorneys - a respected South African law firm known for its commercial expertise - and served in a legal advisory role at Outsurance, developing a sharp understanding of insurance law and corporate risk.

Over more than fifteen years of practice, Masekela has appeared in the High Court on over 308 occasions, building a reputation for rigorous preparation and effective courtroom advocacy. His 82% success rate across contested matters reflects a practice built on strategic thinking and relentless client focus.

His Certificate in Practice Management and PDBM sharpen his ability to run a well-organised, client-centred practice. His MBA - the most recent addition to an already formidable academic record - brings a structured, business-minded approach to legal problem-solving that gives his clients a decisive competitive edge.

Masekela leads all client matters personally. Whether you are a multinational corporation or an individual facing a personal legal crisis, you will deal directly with him - not a junior attorney.
